SHARD CIRCUIT

You’re Juno Hale, ARC’s field jumper. Dr. Roth pulls you off leave to hit Kepler IV-b: the relay team vanished, and an old stack called Cinder is hoarding beacon keys. Clear the lab, pass the training sim, take the portal, then burn through five stages and end Cinder in the core.

How to play

  • Solo: Arrows or WASD to move.
  • Co-op (2 players): P1 WASD + Space to fire; P2 Arrow keys + Right Shift to fire. On phones, use the on-screen sticks and fire buttons.
  • Space fires the sidearm or swings the boss cutter if you’re holding one (solo / P1).
  • P or Esc pauses.
  • Grab enough chips (check the HUD), then run for the exit. In co-op, both players must stand on the exit together.
  • Ice slides · > shove pads · T/U teleporters · C scrap · H extra life · Z speed · R sidearm pickup
  • Sentries shoot in straight lines, so break line of sight or keep moving.
