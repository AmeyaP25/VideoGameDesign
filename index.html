SHARD CIRCUIT

You are Nova. Grab the green shards, dodge traps, beat the Overserver at the end.

How to play

  • Arrows or WASD — move
  • Space — fire when you have a ray gun or the boss plasma saber
  • P / Esc — pause
  • Shards — collect per objective, then reach the exit
  • Ice slides · > boosts · T/U teleporters · C scrap · H life · Z haste · R ray gun pickup
  • Sentries shoot — hide behind walls or keep moving
