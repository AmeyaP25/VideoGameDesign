SHARD CIRCUIT

You’re Juno Hale, the person they call when a job sounds boring until it isn’t. This basement still wants its chips back, so grab the greens, dodge the mess, and put Cinder down in the core.

How to play

  • Arrows or WASD to move.
  • Space fires the sidearm or swings the boss cutter if you’re holding one.
  • P or Esc pauses.
  • Grab enough chips (check the HUD), then run for the exit.
  • Ice slides · > shove pads · T/U teleporters · C scrap · H extra life · Z speed · R sidearm pickup
  • Sentries shoot in straight lines, so break line of sight or keep moving.
